The Founder

Jackie Parker

I have spent my career building things that did not exist yet, inside organizations that needed them to. DeepSight Strategy™ is the next thing I built.

Jackie Parker

Founder · DeepSight Strategy™ · JWP Consulting LLC · Est. 2014

I have spent my career inside some of the most complex organizations in the world, including General Motors, Newell Rubbermaid, and NYSE-listed enterprises. Most of that time, I was the person brought in to build something that did not exist yet, or to fix something that had stopped working.

"I have built the systems leaders are now trying to create, so I know firsthand what they cost, what they take, and where they tend to break. That is what I bring to every engagement."

At Newell Rubbermaid, I founded the corporate foundation and built a $5 million endowment from the ground up. At General Motors, I led the Global Corporate Giving department. The pattern was the same both times: I was recruited into complexity, trusted to build from zero, and expected to deliver at enterprise scale.

I am not a career consultant. I am an operator who advises. That distinction matters more than it sounds. Most advisors show up with a framework and a deck. I show up with pattern recognition earned across industries, organization sizes, and leadership levels, and I stay through execution instead of handing off a binder and leaving.

I founded DeepSight Strategy™ in 2014 under JWP Consulting LLC because I believe leaders navigating the highest-stakes moments of their careers deserve an advisor who has actually operated at their level, not one who has only studied it from the outside.

My advisory work spans CEO transitions, enterprise transformation, AI-enabled operating models, succession planning, board governance, and executive development. I work nationally, with an Atlanta relationship hub, on a remote-first basis.

I am also the founder of The Assignment Room™, a purpose-based coaching and assessment platform for women leaders.
